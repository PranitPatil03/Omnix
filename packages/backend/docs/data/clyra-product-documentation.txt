Clyra — Product Documentation
================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Clyra is an AI-powered contract intelligence platform that helps individuals and teams review, analyze, and understand contracts quickly. Upload any contract PDF, and Clyra's AI provides risk detection, compliance checks, negotiation points, cost analysis, and more.

Clyra is designed for legal professionals, startup founders, procurement teams, freelancers, and anyone who needs to review contracts without spending hours reading through dense legal language.

2. GETTING STARTED
------------------
Step 1: Sign Up
  - Visit https://clyra.ai and click "Get Started"
  - Create a free account with email or sign in with Google/GitHub
  - No credit card required for the Basic plan

Step 2: Upload Your First Contract
  - Click "Upload Contract" or drag and drop a PDF into the upload area
  - Supported format: PDF files only
  - Maximum file size: 50 MB per document
  - Wait for the AI to process (typically 30 seconds to 2 minutes)

Step 3: Review Your Analysis
  - Once processing is complete, you'll see a comprehensive dashboard with:
    • Contract Type Detection (NDA, Employment, Service Agreement, etc.)
    • Risk Assessment with severity levels
    • Opportunities and favorable terms
    • Overall Favorability Score (1-100)
    • Contract Summary

Step 4: Export (Pro Plan)
  - Pro users can export the analysis as PDF or Word document
  - Click "Export Report" and choose your preferred format

5. AI ANALYSIS FEATURES
------------------------

5.1 Risk Detection
  Clyra automatically identifies and categorizes contractual risks by severity:
  - HIGH: Unlimited liability clauses, one-sided termination rights, IP ownership traps, non-compete overreach
  - MEDIUM: Auto-renewal without clear opt-out, vague performance metrics, broad indemnification
  - LOW: Missing standard clauses, ambiguous definitions, minor formatting issues

5.2 Opportunity Detection
  Identifies favorable terms you should be aware of:
  - HIGH IMPACT: Favorable payment terms, strong IP protections for you, generous SLA guarantees
  - MEDIUM IMPACT: Right to audit, reasonable termination notice periods
  - LOW IMPACT: Standard force majeure protections, data backup obligations

5.3 Legal Compliance Check
  Assesses contract against common regulatory requirements:
  - GDPR compliance for data handling
  - CCPA consumer protection alignment
  - SOC 2 security requirements
  - Industry-specific regulations

5.4 Negotiation Recommendations
  AI suggests specific edits and counterproposals:
  - "Consider adding a cap on liability at 2x the contract value"
  - "Request a 30-day cure period before termination for cause"
  - "Add a data deletion clause upon contract termination"

5.5 Contract Summary
  Basic Plan: 2-3 sentence overview
  Pro Plan: Multi-paragraph comprehensive summary covering all key terms, obligations, and notable clauses

8. TROUBLESHOOTING
-------------------

Q: My PDF upload failed. What should I do?
A: Ensure the file is a valid PDF under 50 MB. Scanned documents should have clear, readable text. If the document is image-based, ensure it's at least 150 DPI quality.

Q: The analysis seems incomplete. Why?
A: Some contracts have complex or unusual structures. Try re-uploading. If the issue persists, contact support@clyra.ai with the contract (we'll review confidentially).

Q: How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
A: Go to Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds for the current month.

Q: Can I analyze contracts in languages other than English?
A: Currently, Clyra supports English-language contracts only. Multi-language support (Spanish, French, German, Portuguese) is coming in Q3 2026.

Q: My favorability score seems off. Is it accurate?
A: The favorability score is an AI-generated estimate based on the balance of risks vs. opportunities. It's a helpful indicator but should not replace professional legal review for high-stakes contracts.
