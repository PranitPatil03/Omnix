1. RETURN & REFUND POLICY
--------------------------

1.1 Free Plan (Basic)
  The Basic plan is free forever. No payment is required, no refund applicable.

1.2 Pro Plan — 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee
  - New Pro subscribers are eligible for a full refund within 14 calendar days of their initial upgrade.
  - To request a refund, email support@clyra.ai with your account email and the subject line "Pro Plan Refund Request".
  - Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
  - After the 14-day window, no refunds are issued for the remaining billing period.
  - If you cancel mid-cycle, you retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

1.3 Annual Subscriptions
  - Annual subscribers who request a refund within 14 days of purchase receive a full refund.
  - Annual subscribers who cancel after 14 days can request a prorated refund for unused months. This is calculated as: (Remaining full months / 12) × Annual price paid.
  - Partial months are not refunded.

1.4 Enterprise Plans
  - Enterprise refund terms are governed by individual contract agreements.
  - Early termination fees may apply as specified in the Enterprise service agreement.

1.5 Non-Refundable Items
  - Processing fees for completed contract analyses
  - Custom AI model training fees (Enterprise)
  - API usage charges beyond included limits


2. SUBSCRIPTION MANAGEMENT
----------------------------

2.1 Upgrading
  - Upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time from Account Settings > Billing
  - When upgrading mid-cycle, you're charged the prorated amount for the remaining days in the current billing period
  - Full Pro features are available immediately after upgrade

2.2 Downgrading
  - Downgrade from Pro to Basic from Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription
  - Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period
  - All analyses completed during Pro subscription remain accessible
  - Exported reports (PDF/Word) remain in your Downloads; they are not deleted

2.3 Payment Methods
  - Accepted: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal
  - Currency: USD only (international transactions may incur bank conversion fees)
  - Auto-renewal is enabled by default
  - Failed payment: 3 retry attempts over 7 days. If all fail, account downgrades to Basic and a notification email is sent.

2.4 Invoices & Receipts
  - Monthly invoices are emailed automatically after each payment
  - Historical invoices available at Account Settings > Billing > Invoice History
  - Invoices include: date, amount, plan details, and payment method (last 4 digits)


3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS
-----------------------------

3.1 Platform Availability
  - Target: 99.9% uptime (measured monthly, excluding planned maintenance)
  - Planned maintenance window: Sundays 2:00 AM – 6:00 AM EST (announced 48 hours in advance)
  - Unplanned outages: Status page at status.clyra.ai provides real-time updates

3.2 Analysis Processing Times
  - Basic Plan: Analyses processed within 5 minutes (typical: 1-2 minutes)
  - Pro Plan: Priority processing within 3 minutes (typical: 30-90 seconds)
  - Enterprise Plan: Dedicated processing queue, SLA per contract agreement

3.3 Support Response Times
  - Basic Plan: AI chat support 24/7, no human support SLA
  - Pro Plan: Email support with 24-hour response time (business days), AI chat 24/7
  - Enterprise Plan: Dedicated account manager, 4-hour response for critical issues, 12-hour for standard

3.4 Data Availability
  - Uploaded documents: Available for 90 days from upload date
  - Analysis results: Available indefinitely while account is active
  - Exported reports: Once downloaded, Clyra does not store copies


4. DATA HANDLING & PRIVACY
----------------------------

4.1 Data Collection
  Clyra collects the following data:
  - Account information: name, email, billing details
  - Uploaded documents: contract PDFs for analysis
  - Usage data: timestamps, features used, analysis history
  - Technical data: browser type, IP address (anonymized after 30 days)

4.2 Data Processing
  - Contracts are processed using AI language models hosted on secure, SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  - Processing occurs in US-based data centers (AWS us-east-1 and us-west-2)
  - Documents are temporarily stored in encrypted, isolated processing environments during analysis
  - No human reviews uploaded contracts unless explicitly requested for support purposes

4.3 Data Retention
  - Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  - Uploaded documents: Auto-deleted after 90 days (configurable for Enterprise)
  - Account deletion: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of request
  - Backup retention: Encrypted backups retained for 60 days after deletion for disaster recovery, then permanently destroyed

4.4 Data Sharing
  - Clyra does NOT sell, rent, or share user data with third parties
  - Contract data is NOT used to train AI models
  - Data may be shared with: payment processors (Stripe), email service (SendGrid), and cloud infrastructure (AWS) — all with data processing agreements in place
  - Law enforcement: Data disclosed only with valid court order or subpoena

4.5 GDPR Rights (for EU users)
  - Right to access: Request a copy of all your data via support@clyra.ai
  - Right to rectification: Update incorrect data in Account Settings
  - Right to erasure: Request full account and data deletion
  - Right to portability: Export your analysis data in JSON or CSV format
  - Data Protection Officer: dpo@clyra.ai

4.6 CCPA Rights (for California users)
  - Right to know what personal information is collected
  - Right to delete personal information  
  - Right to opt-out of data sales (Clyra does not sell data)
  - No financial incentive for data sharing


5. ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
--------------------------

5.1 Permitted Use
  - Uploading your own contracts or those you're authorized to review
  - Using analysis results for personal or business decision-making
  - Sharing analysis results with authorized team members (Enterprise)

5.2 Prohibited Use
  - Uploading documents you don't have permission to view or analyze
  - Using Clyra-generated analysis as formal legal advice (we're not a law firm)
  - Automated bulk uploads exceeding fair use (more than 100 documents per day without Enterprise plan)
  - Reverse engineering, scraping, or attempting to extract the AI model
  - Sharing account credentials with unauthorized users
  - Uploading malicious files or content

5.3 Consequences
  - First violation: Warning email with 7-day grace period
  - Second violation: 30-day account suspension
  - Severe violations: Immediate account termination without refund


6. ACCOUNT & BILLING POLICIES
-------------------------------

6.1 Account Creation
  - One account per email address
  - Business email recommended (personal email accepted)
  - Age requirement: 18+ or legal age in your jurisdiction
  - Accurate registration information required

6.2 Account Security
  - Passwords must be 8+ characters with at least one uppercase letter, one number
  - Two-factor authentication (2FA) strongly recommended
  - Account lockout after 5 consecutive failed login attempts (auto-unlock after 30 minutes)
  - Suspicious login detection: Email notification for logins from new devices or locations

6.3 Billing Disputes
  - Contact support@clyra.ai within 30 days of the charge
  - Include transaction date, amount, and reason for dispute
  - Resolution provided within 5 business days
  - Chargebacks filed without contacting Clyra first may result in account suspension


7. SUPPORT TIERS & ESCALATION
-------------------------------

7.1 Tier 1 — AI Chat Support (All Plans)
  - Available: 24/7
  - Handles: General questions, how-to guidance, troubleshooting, account basics
  - Response time: Immediate
  - Languages: English

7.2 Tier 2 — Email Support (Pro & Enterprise)
  - Available: Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM EST
  - Handles: Technical issues, billing questions, analysis accuracy concerns
  - Response time: 24 hours (Pro), 12 hours (Enterprise)
  - Email: support@clyra.ai

7.3 Tier 3 — Dedicated Support (Enterprise Only)
  - Available: Business hours with on-call for critical issues
  - Handles: Integration issues, custom configuration, data requests, escalated incidents
  - Response time: 4 hours (critical), 12 hours (standard)
  - Contact: Dedicated account manager via direct channel

7.4 Escalation Process
  Step 1: Contact Tier 1 or Tier 2 support
  Step 2: If unresolved after 48 hours, request escalation to Tier 3
  Step 3: If still unresolved, request management review via escalation@clyra.ai
  Step 4: Final resolution guaranteed within 10 business days


8. INCIDENT RESPONSE
----------------------

8.1 Incident Severity Levels
  - P1 (Critical): Platform down, data breach, or security vulnerability affecting all users
  - P2 (High): Major feature unavailable, analyses failing for multiple users
  - P3 (Medium): Minor feature degradation, intermittent issues
  - P4 (Low): Cosmetic issues, documentation errors, feature requests

8.2 Response & Resolution Targets
  - P1: Acknowledge within 15 minutes, resolve within 4 hours
  - P2: Acknowledge within 1 hour, resolve within 8 hours
  - P3: Acknowledge within 4 hours, resolve within 48 hours
  - P4: Acknowledge within 24 hours, resolve in next release cycle

8.3 Communication During Incidents
  - Status page: status.clyra.ai (updated every 15 minutes during active incidents)
  - Email notifications: Sent to affected users for P1 and P2 incidents
  - Post-incident report: Published within 72 hours for P1 incidents
